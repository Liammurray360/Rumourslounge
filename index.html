Fred Otto — Owner
This isn't a venue that happens to book live music. It's the room Joburg built for it.

Rumours Lounge has been the room since 12 July 2006 — starting at Palm Court in Welties, then Cresta, Hillfox, Strydom Park, Westgate, and back to Strydom Park for good. Twenty years, five rooms, one crowd that kept following.
